About

Freedom Loader is a lightweight desktop app that wraps yt-dlp to give users an easy, reliable way to download online video and audio. It focuses on three things: control, privacy, and simplicity .

I built this project because I was tired of download tools filled with ads, limits, and paywalls. The frustration of seeing everything driven by profit instead of usefulness. Freedom Loader exists to give you simple offline access to the content you want .
Basically, I just wanted to create the tool I wish I had when I was younger.
